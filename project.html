Patch Vase Collection

A handcrafted clay object designed to live between decoration and function — used as a vase, a candle holder, or a small plant pot.

Role

Design, 3D, Art Direction

Year

2025

Tools

Blender, Photoshop

Concept & Process

The project explores multifunctional decorative objects and the idea of repair as a visible, celebrated element. Each form was designed to feel calm, tactile, and adaptable to modern interiors.

The visual language avoids perfection, focusing instead on texture, material presence, and subtle variation between pieces.
