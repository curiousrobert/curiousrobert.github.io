The Challenge

Launch a premium decorative vase for urban design enthusiasts. The goal was to build brand awareness and drive sales through a high-end visual identity. I needed to ensure total consistency across static and motion assets, using 3D integration to master lighting and material realism.

The Solution

I bridged the gap between product design and marketing by using 3D rendering to achieve a premium look. I crafted a library of assets—from minimalist stills to dynamic motion graphics—that highlighted the vase’s textures. This coherent visual flow guided users from initial awareness to the final click-through.
